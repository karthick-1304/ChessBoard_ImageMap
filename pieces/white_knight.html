About the Piece

The Knight is a unique piece in chess, moving in an 'L' shape: two squares in one direction and then one square perpendicular. It can jump over other pieces.

Powers and Rules

  • Moves in an 'L' shape: two squares in one direction and then one square perpendicular.
  • Can jump over other pieces.
  • Can capture an opponent's piece by landing on its square.
