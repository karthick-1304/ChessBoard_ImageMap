About the Piece

The King is the most important piece in chess. If the King is threatened with capture (check) and cannot escape, it results in checkmate.

Powers and Rules

  • Moves one square in any direction.
  • Cannot move to a square that puts it in check.
  • The game ends in checkmate if the King is captured.
