About the Piece

The Rook is a powerful piece in chess, moving horizontally or vertically across the board. It starts in each corner of the chessboard and has long-range power.

Powers and Rules

  • Moves any number of squares horizontally or vertically.
  • Cannot move diagonally.
  • Can capture an opponent's piece by moving to its square.
