About the Piece

The Pawn is the most numerous piece on the chessboard. It moves forward and captures diagonally. When it reaches the opponent's back rank, it can be promoted to any other piece.

Powers and Rules

  • Moves one square forward.
  • Captures one square diagonally forward.
  • Can move two squares forward from its initial position.
  • Can be promoted to any piece when it reaches the back rank.
