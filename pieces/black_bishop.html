About the Piece

The Bishop is a long-range piece that moves diagonally. It starts next to the King and Queen and has great power across the board.

Powers and Rules

  • Moves diagonally any number of squares.
  • Cannot move horizontally or vertically.
  • Can capture an opponent's piece by landing on its square.
